Research

I am a post-doc in Birte Forstmann’s group at the Cognitive Science Center Amsterdam. I have a background in both psychology and computer science. My current project is at the interface of cognitive modeling and neuroimaging and is concerned with analysing and predicting episodes of mind-wandering (or task-unrelated thoughts) using data from different modalities.
